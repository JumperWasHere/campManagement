Our Programme

FIRST YEAR

In our preschool , children playing together, doing circle time to learn adab & aqidah. Children doing a role play to learn an Islamic life. We aim to take them on an exhilarating journey of learning, growth, and discovery.

SECOND YEAR

Our young kids starts to be encouraged; to be independent, critical thinkers with enquiring minds and the love of learning Islam. Throughout, we emphasise the importance of kindness, consideration and respect and maintain high expectations of behaviour and good manners at all times
